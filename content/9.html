Python and future of AI


Python has been rapidly growing in popularity over the years, especially in the field of artificial intelligence (AI). As a Python student, I have been fascinated by the potential of this versatile programming language and its role in shaping the future of AI.

One of the main reasons why Python has become the go-to language for AI is its simplicity and readability. Its syntax is easy to understand and its vast library of modules and packages make it convenient for developers to implement complex AI algorithms. This allows for faster development
